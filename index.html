About McGuire

Iowa's Most Trusted
Cleaning Company

Since 1986, McGuire Cleaning Service has been the name Algona and Kossuth County residents and business owners turn to when they want the job done right. We're not a franchise — we're a local, family-owned operation that takes pride in every property we clean.

Whether you need a spotless home for your family, a professional environment for your customers, or a thorough deep clean before a big move, we bring nearly four decades of experience and a commitment to results that speak for themselves.
