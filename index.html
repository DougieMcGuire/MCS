About Us

Iowa's Trusted Cleaning Professionals

Since 1986, McGuire Cleaning Service has been the name Algona and Kossuth County residents and business owners turn to when they want the job done right. We're not a franchise — we're a local, family-owned operation that takes pride in every property we clean.

Whether you need a spotless home for your family, a professional environment for your customers, or a deep clean before a big move, we bring decades of experience, top-quality equipment, and a commitment to results that speak for themselves.
